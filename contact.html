FAQ's

What services do you offer at Stuart Marine Ltd? +

We offer comprehensive marine engineering services, including routine engine servicing, full rebuilds, welding, fabrication, and more for both leisure and commercial vessels.

Do you provide mobile repairs or on-site service? +

Yes. We offer a mobile repair service covering North Wales and beyond, fixing issues on-site whenever possible to get you back on the water quickly.

Are you authorized dealers for specific marine engine brands? +

We are independent Volvo Penta Specialists and main agents for various marine engine brands. We also supply genuine parts and new engines with full factory backup.

How can I schedule a service or request a quote? +

You can fill out our contact form above or call us directly. We’ll discuss your needs and arrange a convenient time to assess or service your vessel.

What if my vessel needs major repairs or a full engine overhaul? +

Our experienced engineers can handle complete overhauls and complex repairs. We follow manufacturer guidelines and invest in modern equipment to ensure top-quality workmanship.

Do you handle electrical and GRP repairs? +

Yes, we can tackle electrical issues and GRP work. From diagnosing wiring problems to repairing fiberglass damage, our team has the expertise and tools to handle it all.
