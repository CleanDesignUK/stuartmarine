Our Services

Just like motor cars, boat engines require routine servicing and preventative maintenance. Stuart Marine offers a comprehensive range of service routines covering all engine types—diesel, petrol inboard, outboard, and stern drives.

We also perform spring commissioning and winter decommissioning. Our service packages are tailored to suit the requirements of your vessel, from Basic service to Platinum packages.

Basic Service Schedule

  • Replace fuel filters
  • Oil & filter change
  • Commission the engine with serviced parts

Gold Service Schedule

  • Includes all in the Basic Service
  • Renew Transmission Fluids
  • Freshwater Cooling System drain & refill
  • Battery installation & inspection
  • Engine tuning & commissioning

Platinum Service Schedule

  • Full out / in water service
  • Electrical system inspection
  • Anodes inspection & replacement
  • Sea cocks service & replacement
  • All Basic & Gold package inclusions

Engine Repairs & New Installations

We carry out all manner of engine repairs, from minor fixes to major overhauls. Our workshop is equipped to handle diesel engines, petrol inboards, and outboards, following the relevant manufacturer guidelines.

Stuart Marine is a main dealer for Beta Marine and Mermaid Marine. We can also supply a full range of Volvo Penta engines and handle Mercruiser servicing. Our new engine installations include careful evaluation of the vessel, ensuring safe placement and operation.

Dealerships & Partnerships

  • Main Agents for Mermaid Marine, Baudoui, Sole Marine, Hyundai Seasall, Selva Outboards, Hertz Marine Audio
  • Registered Beta Marine installers
  • Independent Volvo Penta Specialist
  • Griffin Filters
  • OXE Marine outboards (diesel powered)

We will gladly source the necessary parts and components you need. Ask us about any specific engine brand or setup you require.

Refit, Repower & More

From bow thruster installations to repowering vessels with diesel engines, we have documented numerous successful projects—serving both commercial operators and leisure boat owners. Our team can tackle hydraulic problems, pot hauler repairs, and more.
