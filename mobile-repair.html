Mobile Repair & Servicing

Our Mobile Repair Services

Our Mobile Repair team at Stuart Marine provides on-site solutions for boat engines and related systems whether you’re experiencing engine problems or electrical issues and any other marine engineering related problems. Our technicians will travel to your vessels location minimising downtime and maximising convenience. We also offer mobile diagnostics service with our Jaltest multibrand diagnostics. Engine models covered (this list is not exhaustive); Volvo Penta, Yanmar, Mercruiser, Cummins, Caterpillar, FPT, Honda, Suzuki, Yamaha, Mercury, Mariner.

Why Choose Stuart Marine?

Mobile Repairs

Our mobile service saves you time and effort by bringing marine servicing directly to your vessel.

Expert Technicians

Our technicians can carry out minor repairs to advances engine replacement/refits. Our fully equipped workshop can handle vessels up to 45 feet in length and 13 foot beam allowing us to carry out repairs under cover in our secure and heated workshop.
