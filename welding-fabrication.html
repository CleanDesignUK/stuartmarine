Our Welding & Fabrication Solutions

At Stuart Marine, our Welding & Fabrication department handles everything from minor hull repairs to advanced structural builds. Whether it’s reinforcing a propeller shaft bracket, adding a custom platform for a bow thruster, or performing general marine servicing, we adhere to strict Volvo Penta company guidelines to ensure your boat engines remain secure and optimized.

Our skilled fabricators work with aluminum, stainless steel, and other marine-grade materials, delivering long-lasting, corrosion-resistant results that keep your vessel seaworthy and efficient.
